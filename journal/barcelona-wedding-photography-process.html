How to Reach Me

Website contact form: The most common way couples find me Instagram DM: Great for quick questions and vibe-checking Email: For detailed inquiries and attachments

WhatsApp: For international couples who prefer instant messaging

Referral: Many of my couples come from past clients, planners, or venues

What Happens Next

Within 24 hours (usually within 2 to 4 hours): - I respond personally, no auto-replies, no assistants - I confirm my availability for your date - I send my initial package information and pricing - I invite you to schedule a consultation call If I’m unavailable: - I recommend 2 to 3 trusted Barcelona photographers who might be a better fit - I explain why I think they’d suit your style or needs - I stay connected, sometimes dates change, and I’d love to work with you if they do What couples say: “I contacted five photographers, and you were the only one who responded within hours with a personal, thoughtful message. That told me everything I needed to know about how you work.”

Before the Call

You receive a brief questionnaire about your wedding details, priorities, and vision I review your answers and prepare relevant galleries, venue examples, and timeline suggestions I research your venue (if I haven’t shot there before) to offer specific insights

During the Call (30 to 45 Minutes)

My agenda: 1. Your story: How you met, what you love about each other, why Barcelona 2. Your vision: What matters most to you about your wedding photography 3. Your venue: Logistics, light, locations, challenges, opportunities 4. My approach: How I work, what you can expect, my style and philosophy 5. Your questions: Everything on your mind, pricing, timeline, deliverables, concerns 6. Next steps: If we’re a good fit, I explain the booking process What I bring: - Full galleries from weddings similar to yours - Venue-specific insights and portrait location suggestions - A preliminary timeline based on your ceremony time and season - Honest answers, even when the answer is “that’s not my specialty, but here’s who is”

After the Call

Within 24 hours: - Follow-up email with a summary of our conversation - Custom quote based on your specific needs - Relevant galleries and resources - Contract and retainer information (if you want to move forward)

The Contract

My contract is clear, comprehensive, and fair. It covers: - Services: Exactly what’s included in your package - Timeline: Date, hours of coverage, key details - Payment: Retainer amount, payment schedule, accepted methods - Cancellation: Policies for both parties, rescheduling options - Deliverables: Number of photos, delivery timeline, album details (if applicable) - Rights: Print release, usage rights, social media sharing - Backup plans: What happens if I’m unable to attend (extremely rare, but covered) No hidden clauses. No surprises.

The Retainer

Amount: 30% of the total package price Purpose: Secures your date exclusively, I don’t double-book Payment: Bank transfer, credit card, or international transfer (for overseas couples) Confirmation: Once received, your date is officially reserved

The Planning Begins

Immediately after booking: - You receive my Barcelona Wedding Photography Guide, a comprehensive resource covering: - Timeline planning tips - Portrait location recommendations - Vendor suggestions (planners, florists, hair/makeup, videographers) - Barcelona-specific logistics - What to wear for engagement sessions - Rain and weather contingency plans 2 to 3 months before the wedding: - Venue walkthrough (if possible): I visit your venue to scout light, angles, and backup locations - Timeline collaboration: I work with your planner (or directly with you) to build a photography-friendly timeline - Portrait location selection: We choose 2 to 3 locations based on your style, venue, and the season’s light

Why the Engagement Session Matters

The engagement session isn’t just a “bonus”, it’s a strategic part of your wedding photography experience: Builds comfort: You get used to being in front of my camera Tests our working relationship: We learn how to communicate and collaborate Creates beautiful images: For save-the-dates, wedding websites, guest books Informs the wedding day: I learn your best angles, your natural interactions, your comfort zones

How It Works

Before the session: - We choose a location (or 2 to 3 locations) based on your style - I send outfit guidance and styling tips - We confirm timing based on light and weather During the session (1.5 to 2.5 hours): - We meet at the location, chat, and ease into it - I start with simple prompts and build to more dynamic shots - We explore different backdrops, lights, and moods - We have fun, this isn’t a test, it’s a celebration After the session: - Sneak peek: 5 to 10 images within 1 week - Full gallery: 80 to 120 edited images within 2 to 3 weeks - Online gallery with download and sharing capabilities What couples say: “I was terrified before the engagement session. By the end, I was laughing and completely forgot about the camera.

The wedding day felt effortless because we’d already done this.”

The Final Check-In

Video call or detailed email covering: - Final timeline confirmation - Family formal shot list (if applicable) - Special details or surprises to capture - Vendor contact information - Emergency contacts - Weather contingency plans - Any last-minute changes or concerns

What I Do Behind the Scenes

Equipment check: All cameras, lenses, flashes, memory cards, batteries tested and packed Backup preparation: Secondary equipment charged, formatted, and ready Route planning: GPS routes to all locations, parking research, traffic timing Vendor coordination: I reach out to your planner, videographer, and venue coordinator to confirm logistics Weather monitoring: I track forecasts daily and adjust plans as needed

Before I Arrive

15 to 30 minutes early: I arrive before my start time to scout, set up, and say hello Venue assessment: I check light conditions, identify any changes, and adjust my plan Equipment setup: Cameras ready, backup accessible, flash tested Throughout the Day My approach: - Documentary foundation: I observe and capture real moments - Editorial direction: During portraits, I guide you with gentle prompts - Traditional efficiency: Family formals are organized and swift - Constant communication: With your planner, venue staff, and other vendors - Adaptability: If the timeline shifts, I shift with it, no stress, no panic What you’re experiencing: - Minimal interruption from me - Occasional gentle direction during portraits - The feeling that I’m a trusted presence, not a hovering vendor - Confidence that everything is being captured

After the Last Dance

Equipment packed carefully: Memory cards secured, cameras protected Quick goodbye: I thank you, congratulate you, and let you celebrate No intrusion: I don’t ask for final shots or keep you from your guests

Phase 7: Post-Production (Days 1 to 42)

Day 1: Immediate Backup Dual memory cards: Every photo is saved to two cards during the wedding Cloud upload: Within 24 hours, all images are backed up to secure cloud storage Local backup: Copies on multiple hard drives Message to you: “Your photos are safe.

Sneak peek coming soon.” Days 2 to 7: Culling & Selection Initial review: I go through every image (typically 3,000 to 5,000 raw files) Selection: I choose the best 600 to 900 images that tell your complete story Organization: Images sorted by category (getting ready, ceremony, portraits, reception) Sneak peek selection: I choose 10 to 15 of my favorites for a quick preview Days 5 to 7: Sneak Peek Delivery 10 to 15 edited images: My favorite moments from the day Perfect for social media: Sized and optimized for Instagram, Facebook, etc.

For sharing: Send to family, post to wedding websites, use for thank-you cards Weeks 2 to 6: Full Editing My editing process: - Color grading: Every image receives my signature warm, timeless color palette - Exposure adjustment: Perfecting highlights, shadows, and contrast - Skin retouching: Natural, not overdone, you look like you, just your best you - Cropping and composition: Ensuring every image is visually strong - Black and white conversions: Selected images converted for emotional impact - Consistency: The entire gallery has a unified look and feel What this means: - No batch editing: Every image receives individual attention - No AI filters: Real editing by a real human who was at your wedding - No shortcuts: Quality takes time, and your photos deserve it

The Online Gallery

500 to 800 fully edited, high-resolution images (depending on package and coverage) Private, password-protected gallery, share with family, keep from the public Download options: Full resolution for printing, web-sized for sharing Favorites feature: You and your guests can mark favorite images Social sharing: Easy sharing to Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest Print release included: Print wherever you like, as large as you like

The Experience

I recommend viewing your gallery over several days, not in one sitting. Let the emotions settle. Notice the moments you forgot. Discover the details you missed. The gallery is designed to be experienced, not just viewed. What couples say: “I cried three times going through our gallery. Not just at the big moments, at the tiny ones I didn’t even know happened. Our photographer saw everything.”

Phase 9: Album Design (Week 6 to 14)

If You Ordered an Album Week 6 to 8: - You select your favorite 60 to 100 images (or I select based on our consultation) - I begin designing your album, following a narrative structure Week 8 to 10: - First draft delivered digitally - We schedule a video call to review the design together - You can swap images, adjust layouts, add or remove spreads Week 10 to 12: - Revisions completed based on your feedback - Final design approved - Sent to my trusted album lab for production Week 14 to 16: - Album arrives - I deliver it personally (when possible) or ship it insured with tracking - The moment you hold it, that’s when it becomes real

Phase 10: The Relationship Continues

Beyond the Wedding My relationship with couples doesn’t end when the album arrives. Many of my past clients: - Return for anniversary sessions: In Barcelona or wherever life takes them - Refer friends and family: The highest compliment I can receive - Stay in touch: Through Instagram, email, or occasional coffee when they’re in town - Share their lives: I love seeing babies, new homes, career milestones

The Long View

Your wedding photos aren’t just for today. They’re for: - Your first anniversary: When you pull out the album and relive the day - Your fifth anniversary: When you notice details you missed the first time - Your tenth anniversary: When you show the photos to your children - Your fiftieth anniversary: When the photos are more precious than ever I’m not just your wedding photographer. I’m the person who helps you hold onto this moment forever.

FAQ
How far in advance should we book you? For peak season (May to October), 12 to 18 months is ideal. For off-season or elopements, 6 to 9 months is usually sufficient. Popular dates (June Saturdays, September weekends) book up fastest.

Can we book you without a consultation? Technically yes, but I don’t recommend it. The consultation ensures we’re a good fit, personality, style, vision. It’s as much for you as it is for me.

What if we need to reschedule our wedding? Life happens. My contract includes rescheduling options. If your new date is available, we transfer your booking at no extra cost. If I’m unavailable, I help you find a replacement photographer.

How do we access our online gallery? You receive a private link and password. The gallery is accessible from any device, phone, tablet, computer. You can download images, share the link with family, and mark favorites.

Can we order prints through you? Yes. I offer professional printing services with archival-quality materials. However, your print release allows you to print anywhere you like, at home, at a local lab, or through my professional service.

Do you keep our photos forever? I archive wedding galleries for a minimum of 2 years. After that, I recommend you keep your own backups. I can provide raw files or additional copies upon request.

What if we want additional editing after delivery? Minor adjustments (exposure, cropping) are included. Extensive retouching or creative edits can be arranged at an additional cost. I’m always happy to discuss your vision.
