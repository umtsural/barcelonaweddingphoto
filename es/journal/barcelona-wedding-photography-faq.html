Cuándo should we book our fotógrafo de bodas en Barcelona?

For peak season (May to October): Book 12 to 18 months in advance. The best fotógrafos are typically booked 1 to 2 years out for Saturday bodas in June, July, and September. For off-peak season (November to March): 6 to 9 months is usually sufficient, though popular dates around Christmas and Valentine's Day fill up quickly. For elopements and micro-bodas: 3 to 6 months ahead often works, especially for weekday ceremonies. My availability: I take a limited number of bodas each year to ensure every pareja receives my full attention. If you're considering me, reach out early, even if your date isn't finalized yet. Do you travel outside Barcelona for bodas? Absolutely.

While I'm based in Barcelona, I regularly photograph bodas across: Costa Brava (Tossa de Mar, Cadaqués, Begur) Sitges & Garraf Coast Mallorca & Ibiza Madrid, Seville, San Sebastián International destinations (Italy, France, Portugal, Greece) Travel fees are calculated transparently based on distance and accommodation needs. For bodas within 1 hour of Barcelona, travel is typically included in my packages. Can we meet before booking? Yes, and I encourage it. I offer complimentary video calls for all parejas considering me.

This isn't a sales pitch; it's a chance for us to: Discuss your vision and priorities Review my full galerías (not just Instagram highluzs) Talk through your Barcelona espacio and timing See if our personalities click The best photos happen when you trust your fotógrafo. A 20-minute call tells us both if we're the right fit.

Qué happens if you're sick or can't make it?

This is a pregunta every pareja should ask every fotógrafo.

My backup system:

Equipment backup: I carry two professional camera bodies, multiple lenses, and redundant memory cards to every boda Photographer backup: I'm part of a trusted network of 5 fotógrafo de bodas en Barcelonas. If an emergency prevents me from attending (this has never happened, but I'm prepared), I have a qualified replacement who matches my estilo and quality standards Image backup: All photos are backed up to dual memory cards during the boda and to cloud storage within 24 hours Insurance: Full professional liability and equipment insurance

During prep: I'm a quiet observer, capturing natural moments, laughter, tears, the dress going on

During the ceremonia: I position myself discreetly, using silent shutter modes. You'll barely notice me During retratos: I guide you with gentle direction, "walk toward me and look at each other", so you feel natural, not posed

Cómo long until we receive our photos?

Sneak peek: 5 to 7 days after the boda (10 to 15 images for social media) Full galería: 4 to 6 weeks for standard entrega Rush entrega: 2 weeks available for an additional fee Each image is individually edited for color, exposure, and consistency with my signature estilo. I don't batch-edit or use AI filters, every photo receives personal attention.

My packages include high-resolution, fully edited JPEGs with full print rights. Raw files are not included, as they represent unfinished work. Qué's your fotografía estilo?

I describe my estilo as "documental soul with editorial eyes." Documentary: I believe the best moments are unscripted. The tear your mother wipes away. The way your partner looks at you when you aren't watching. The spontaneous dance move during the recepción. Editorial: Cuándo we do retratos, I draw on fashion fotografía techniques, beautiful luz, thoughtful composition, and posing that feels natural but looks stunning. My color palette is warm and timeless, with true-to-life skin tones and a soft, romantic quality that doesn't feel over-processed. Do you shoot film or digital? I shoot 100% digital with professional Sony mirrorless cameras.

While I love the aesthetic of film, digital technology has advanced to the point where I can achieve a film-like quality with greater reliability, especially important for once-in-a-lifetime moments. For parejas who specifically want film fotografía, I can arrange a hybrid package with a trusted film specialist as a segundo fotógrafo.

Cómo do you handle Barcelona's tourist crowds?

Barcelona is a busy city, and popular photo locations like Park Güell and the Gothic Quarter can be packed. My strategies: Timing: We shoot at sunrise or during golden hour when crowds are thinner

Hidden gems: I know quiet corners, side streets, and lesser-known viewpoints that tourists miss

Patience: Sometimes we wait 2 minutes for a group to pass. It's worth it for a clean shot Embracing the energy: Sometimes the crowd becomes part of the story, a Barcelona boda photo with life happening around you can be magical

Qué if it rains on our boda day?

Barcelona enjoys 300+ sunny days per year, but rain happens, especially in October and April. My approach: Embrace it: Rainy boda photos can be incredibly romantic and unique Indoor locations: I scout covered options at every espacio (arcades, courtyards, elegant interiors) Umbrellas: Clear umbrellas make for gorgeous, dreamy retratos Flexibility: If the forecast shifts, we adjust the timing to catch breaks in the weather

Cómo do we book you?

Reach out via my contact form with your date and espacio Schedule a call to discuss your vision and see if we're a good fit Review the contract, I use clear, straightforward agreements with no hidden clauses Pay the retainer (30% of the package precio) to secure your date Sign the contract, your date is officially reserved!

The remaining balance is due 2 weeks before the boda.

Do you offer payment plans? Yes. While the standard structure is 30% retainer + 70% final payment, I can split the balance into 3 to 4 installments for parejas who prefer. Just let me know during our call.

Qué's your cancellation policy?

Life happens. My policy is: More than 6 months before: Retainer refunded minus a €300 administrative fee 3 to 6 months before: 50% of retainer refunded Less than 3 months before: Retainer retained, but transferable to a new date within 12 months (subject to availability) I always try to be flexible and understanding with unexpected circumstances.
