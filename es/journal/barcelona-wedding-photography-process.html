Cómo to Reach Me

Website contact form: The most common way parejas find me Instagram DM: Great for quick preguntas and vibe-checking Email: For detailed inquiries and attachments

QuésApp: For international parejas who prefer instant messaging

Referral: Many of my parejas come from past clients, planners, or espacios

Qué Happens Next

Within 24 hours (usually within 2 to 4 hours): - I respond personally, no auto-replies, no assistants - I confirm my availability for your date - I send my initial package information and precios - I invite you to schedule a consultation call If I’m unavailable: - I recommend 2 to 3 trusted Barcelona fotógrafos who might be a better fit - I explain why I think they’d suit your estilo or needs - I stay connected, sometimes dates change, and I’d love to work with you if they do Qué parejas say: “I contacted five fotógrafos, and you were the only one who responded within hours with a personal, thoughtful message. That told me everything I needed to know about how you work.”

Before the Call

You receive a brief preguntanaire about your boda details, priorities, and vision I review your answers and prepare relevant galerías, espacio examples, and timing suggestions I research your espacio (if I haven’t shot there before) to offer specific insights

During the Call (30 to 45 Minutes)

My agenda: 1. Your story: Cómo you met, what you love about each other, why Barcelona 2. Your vision: Qué matters most to you about your boda fotografía 3. Your espacio: Logistics, luz, locations, challenges, opportunities 4. My approach: Cómo I work, what you can expect, my estilo and philosophy 5. Your preguntas: Everything on your mind, precios, timing, deliverables, concerns 6. Next steps: If we’re a good fit, I explain the booking process Qué I bring: - Full galerías from bodas similar to yours - Venue-specific insights and retrato location suggestions - A preliminary timing based on your ceremonia time and season - Honest answers, even when the answer is “that’s not my specialty, but here’s who is”

After the Call

Within 24 hours: - Follow-up email with a summary of our conversation - Custom quote based on your specific needs - Relevant galerías and resources - Contract and retainer information (if you want to move forward)

The Contract

My contract is clear, comprehensive, and fair. It covers: - Services: Exactly what’s included in your package - Timeline: Date, hours of coverage, key details - Payment: Retainer amount, payment schedule, accepted methods - Cancellation: Policies for both parties, rescheduling options - Deliverables: Number of photos, entrega timing, álbum details (if applicable) - Rights: Print release, usage rights, social media sharing - Backup plans: Qué happens if I’m unable to attend (extremely rare, but covered) No hidden clauses. No surprises.

The Retainer

Amount: 30% of the total package precio Purpose: Secures your date exclusively, I don’t double-book Payment: Bank transfer, credit card, or international transfer (for overseas parejas) Confirmation: Once received, your date is officially reserved

The Planificación Begins

Immediately after booking: - You receive my Barcelona Fotografía de boda Guía, a comprehensive resource covering: - Timeline planning tips - Portrait location recommendations - Vendor suggestions (planners, florists, hair/makeup, videographers) - Barcelona-specific logistics - Qué to wear for sesión de parejas - Rain and weather contingency plans 2 to 3 months before the boda: - Venue walkthrough (if possible): I visit your espacio to scout luz, angles, and backup locations - Timeline collaboration: I work with your planner (or directly with you) to build a fotografía-friendly timing - Portrait location selection: We choose 2 to 3 locations based on your estilo, espacio, and the season’s luz

Por qué the Engagement Session Matters

The sesión de pareja isn’t just a “bonus”, it’s a strategic part of your boda fotografía experiencia: Builds comfort: You get used to being in front of my camera Tests our working relationship: We learn how to communicate and collaborate Creates beautiful images: For save-the-dates, boda websites, guest books Informs the boda day: I learn your best angles, your natural interactions, your comfort zones

Cómo It Works

Before the session: - We choose a location (or 2 to 3 locations) based on your estilo - I send outfit guidance and styling tips - We confirm timing based on luz and weather During the session (1.5 to 2.5 hours): - We meet at the location, chat, and ease into it - I start with simple prompts and build to more dynamic shots - We explore different backdrops, luzs, and moods - We have fun, this isn’t a test, it’s a celebration After the session: - Sneak peek: 5 to 10 images within 1 week - Full galería: 80 to 120 edited images within 2 to 3 weeks - Online galería with download and sharing capabilities Qué parejas say: “I was terrified before the sesión de pareja. By the end, I was laughing and completely forgot about the camera.

The boda day felt effortless because we’d already done this.”

The Final Check-In

Video call or detailed email covering: - Final timing confirmation - Family formal shot list (if applicable) - Special details or surprises to capture - Vendor contact information - Emergency contacts - Weather contingency plans - Any last-minute changes or concerns

Qué I Do Behind the Scenes

Equipment check: All cameras, lenses, flashes, memory cards, batteries tested and packed Backup preparation: Secondary equipment charged, formatted, and ready Route planning: GPS routes to all locations, parking research, traffic timing Vendor coordination: I reach out to your planner, videographer, and espacio coordinator to confirm logistics Weather monitoring: I track forecasts daily and adjust plans as needed

Before I Arrive

15 to 30 minutes early: I arrive before my start time to scout, set up, and say hello Venue assessment: I check luz conditions, identify any changes, and adjust my plan Equipment setup: Cameras ready, backup accessible, flash tested Throughout the Day My approach: - Documentary foundation: I observe and capture real moments - Editorial direction: During retratos, I guide you with gentle prompts - Traditional efficiency: Family formals are organized and swift - Constant communication: With your planner, espacio staff, and other vendors - Adaptability: If the timing shifts, I shift with it, no stress, no panic Qué you’re experiencing: - Minimal interruption from me - Occasional gentle direction during retratos - The feeling that I’m a trusted presence, not a hovering vendor - Confidence that everything is being captured

After the Last Dance

Equipment packed carefully: Memory cards secured, cameras protected Quick goodbye: I thank you, congratulate you, and let you celebrate No intrusion: I don’t ask for final shots or keep you from your guests

The Online Gallery

500 to 800 fully edited, high-resolution images (depending on package and coverage) Private, password-protected galería, share with family, keep from the public Download options: Full resolution for printing, web-sized for sharing Favorites feature: You and your guests can mark favorite images Social sharing: Easy sharing to Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest Print release included: Print wherever you like, as large as you like

The Experience

I recommend viewing your galería over several days, not in one sitting. Let the emotions settle. Notice the moments you forgot. Discover the details you missed. The galería is designed to be experienciad, not just viewed. Qué parejas say: “I cried three times going through our galería. Not just at the big moments, at the tiny ones I didn’t even know happened. Our fotógrafo saw everything.”

Phase 9: Album Design (Week 6 to 14)

If You Ordered an Album Week 6 to 8: - You select your favorite 60 to 100 images (or I select based on our consultation) - I begin designing your álbum, following a narrative structure Week 8 to 10: - First draft delivered digitally - We schedule a video call to review the design together - You can swap images, adjust layouts, add or remove spreads Week 10 to 12: - Revisions completed based on your feedback - Final design approved - Sent to my trusted álbum lab for production Week 14 to 16: - Album arrives - I deliver it personally (when possible) or ship it insured with tracking - The moment you hold it, that’s when it becomes real

Many of my past clients: - Return for anniversary sessions: In Barcelona or wherever life takes them - Refer friends and family: The highest compliment I can receive - Stay in touch: Through Instagram, email, or occasional coffee when they’re in town - Share their lives: I love seeing babies, new homes, career milestones

Preguntas frecuentes
Cómo far in advance should we book you? For peak season (May to October), 12 to 18 months is ideal. For off-season or elopements, 6 to 9 months is usually sufficient. Popular dates (June Saturdays, September weekends) book up fastest.

Can we book you without a consultation? Technically yes, but I don’t recommend it. The consultation ensures we’re a good fit, personality, estilo, vision. It’s as much for you as it is for me.

Qué if we need to reschedule our boda? Life happens. My contract includes rescheduling options. If your new date is available, we transfer your booking at no extra coste. If I’m unavailable, I help you find a replacement fotógrafo.

Cómo do we access our online galería? You receive a private link and password. The galería is accessible from any device, phone, tablet, computer. You can download images, share the link with family, and mark favorites.

Can we order prints through you? Yes. I offer professional printing services with archival-quality materials. Cómoever, your print release allows you to print anywhere you like, at home, at a local lab, or through my professional service.

Do you keep our photos forever? I archive boda galerías for a minimum of 2 years. After that, I recommend you keep your own backups. I can provide raw files or additional copies upon request.

Qué if we want additional editing after entrega? Minor adjustments (exposure, cropping) are included. Extensive retouching or creative edits can be arranged at an additional coste. I’m always happy to discuss your vision.
