Hours of Coverage

This is the biggest factor. Most Barcelona bodas need: 6 hours: Ceremony + recepción only 8 hours: Getting ready through first dance 10 to 12 hours: Full story from morning prep to late-night dancing Multi-day: Welcome dinner, boda day, brunch, popular for destination bodas

Second Shooter

A second fotógrafo adds €400 to €800 but captures: Groom prep while the main fotógrafo is with the bride Different angles during the ceremonia Candid guest moments during cocktail hour For bodas over 100 guests, I strongly recommend a segundo fotógrafo.

Engagement / Pre-Wedding Session

Many Barcelona fotógrafos include this in packages. Benefits: You get comfortable in front of the camera We find your best angles and luzing You receive beautiful photos for save-the-dates or boda websites We build rapport before the big day Typical coste if separate: €300 to €600

Wedding Album

Not all fotógrafos offer álbums, but a handcrafted, lay-flat álbum is an heirloom that lasts generations.

Travel & Accommodation

For destination bodas outside Barcelona: Sitges / Costa Brava: Usually included or small fee, €100 to €300 Madrid / Seville / Ibiza: Travel + 1 night accommodation International: Full travel costes + 2 to 3 nights

Editing Style & Turnaround

Premium fotógrafos invest significant time in: Color grading for a consistent, signature look Skin retouching, natural, not overdone Culling 3,000+ raw images down to the best 500 to 800 Gallery design with professional presentation Typical entrega: 4 to 8 weeks for full galería. Rush entrega, 2 weeks, may add €200 to €500.

Cómo to Budget for Photography

Most boda planners recommend allocating 10 to 15% of your total boda budget to fotografía. For a €30,000 Barcelona boda, that's €3,000 to €4,500. If fotografía is a priority for you, consider reducing floral budget sluzly, choosing a Friday or Sunday boda for espacio discounts, or opting for a smaller, more curated guest list.

Preguntas frecuentes
Por qué do fotógrafo de bodas en Barcelonas coste more than in other Spanish cities?

Barcelona is Spain's most popular boda destination. Higher demand, international clientele, and the coste of living in the city all contribute to precios. You're also paying for expertise in iconic locations like Park Güell and the Gothic Quarter.

Can I negotiate with boda fotógrafos?

Some fotógrafos offer flexibility for off-peak dates, November to March, or weekday bodas. Cómoever, most established fotógrafos have fixed precios. Instead of negotiating, ask about payment plans or customized packages.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. I typically require a 30% retainer to secure your date, with the remaining balance due 2 weeks before the boda. For parejas who prefer, I can split payments into 3 to 4 installments.

Qué's the difference between a €2,000 and a €5,000 fotógrafo?

Experience, consistency, editing quality, equipment, backup systems, professionalism, and the overall experiencia. A higher-preciod fotógrafo has likely shot 100+ bodas, has refined their craft, and delivers a seamless, stress-free experiencia.

Do you travel outside Barcelona?

Absolutely. I regularly shoot in Sitges, Costa Brava, Mallorca, and across Europe. Travel costes are calculated transparently and included in your custom quote.
